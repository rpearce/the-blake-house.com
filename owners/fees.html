Usage Fees & Assessments

Fees for 2022 will be $70 per day for 10 or fewer people and $90 per day for 11 or more people. When you send in your fees, please indicate on the check which days you are paying for. Thanks!

Quarterly assessments will be $2400 per family per quarter for the first three quarters. Quarterly payments are due on the 15th of February, May, August, and November. Please write "(month) assessment" at the bottom of these checks. Thanks again!
